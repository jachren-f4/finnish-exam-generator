Summary

17 total variants to compare, with 7 iterations of Variant 1 (the recommended family) plus 10 alternative approaches.

Variant 1 family (1, 1a-1f) all use the collapsed details pattern with different visual treatments. Variants 8-11 explore more creative approaches including no details section, integrated badges, info strips, and animations.

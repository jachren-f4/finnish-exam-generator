Variant 7: No Display (Pure Focus)

Philosophy: Don't show previous answers at all during retake. Encode status in progress dots (green=correct, red=wrong). Maximum focus on new attempt. Previous answers shown only in final results comparison.

Rationale: Seeing the previous answer might bias the student. Pure retake experience lets them genuinely test if they learned the material.
